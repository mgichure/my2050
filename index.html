that can be used to predict Kenya's CO2 greenhouse gas emissions to inform the necessary
decisions that will help realise Kenyas journey to net zero emission targets by 2050.

My2050 Kenya

Both versions contain levers of decarbonisation, 15 in My2050 Kenya and 45 in the detailed
version. You select your level of ambition of decarbonisation effort using the levers, ranging
from Level 1 - minimal effort, to Level 4 - maximum effort. Popup descriptions explain what
the levels represent in terms of behavioural change or infrastructure investment.

The levels of ambition

The levels of ambition are based on views expressed by over 100 expert stakeholders, including academics, non-government organisations, industry bodies, operators and consultants. They don't reflect government policy but can be used to represent policy.
